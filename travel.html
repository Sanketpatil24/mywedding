AMS → BOM → PNQ
Alternative via Mumbai: If flying into Mumbai (BOM), the expressway drive to Pune is ~3 hours. Shatabdi Express train takes ~3.5h. Pre-book Ola Outstation for comfort.
Tips for Amsterdam travellers
Arrive in Pune by the evening of 23 Nov to have a full day's rest before the Haldi.
India requires a valid passport with 6+ months validity and a tourist visa (e-Visa available online at indianvisaonline.gov.in).
Indian rupees (INR) are the local currency. Carry some cash for autos and small purchases.

Once you're in Pune

Getting around

🚗
Ola & Uber
Both work great in Pune. Download the apps before you land. Pre-book for the wedding days to avoid surge pricing.
🛺
Auto-rickshaws
Excellent for short trips around the city. Agree on the fare before getting in, or ask for the meter.
📍
Marigold Banquets
Save the Google Maps location to your phone. Share it with your cab driver — most will know the area.
