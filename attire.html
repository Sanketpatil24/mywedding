Wedding & reception attire

The main day calls for your most beautiful outfit. Traditional Indian formal wear is the star of the show, but Western formal is equally welcome.

Silk sarees, heavy lehengas, formal anarkali suits, or a chic formal dress — all are perfect.

Wedding & reception attire

Sherwani is the traditional choice and looks stunning. A formal suit or blazer with trousers is equally appropriate and appreciated.

For the reception, semi-formal is also welcome — smart trousers with a formal kurta or blazer works perfectly.

General guidance

Good to know

👟
Footwear tip
You'll be asked to remove shoes when entering certain ceremonial areas. Slip-on footwear or sandals make this much easier.
🌤️
November weather
Pune in November is lovely — around 18–28°C. Light fabrics like georgette, chiffon, and cotton silk are perfect. No need for heavy layers.
📸
Dress for photos
There will be lots of photos! Wear colours that photograph beautifully — jewel tones, pastels, and bright colours all look stunning in Indian wedding photography.
