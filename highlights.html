Day 1 · Monday, 24 November

Haldi & Sangeet

4pm
Until midnight
🌿
Haldi · 4:00 PM
Haldi Ceremony

A joyful turmeric ritual to bless and beautify the couple before their wedding. Turmeric paste is applied by family and friends amid laughter, music, and colour.

🕓 4:00 PM · Marigold Banquets
🎶
Sangeet · Evening
Sangeet Night

An evening of music, dance, and performances. Family and friends perform songs and dances for the couple. A night of pure joy and celebration.

🕖 Evening · Marigold Banquets
🍽️
Dinner · Night
Celebratory Dinner

A festive dinner with family and friends. Food, laughter, and the first toast to the couple as the evening continues until midnight.

🕙 Night · Until 12am
👗
What to wear — Day 1
Haldi: Light, bright cotton in yellow, orange, or white. You will get turmeric on you — dress accordingly! Old clothes you love.
Sangeet: Festive and colourful. Lehenga, saree, or anarkali for women. Kurta-pyjama or sherwani for men.
Yellow Orange Pink Green

💡 Tips for Day 1

Arrive by 3:45 PM for the Haldi — it starts promptly at 4:00 PM.
Wear clothes you don't mind getting turmeric on. Bring a change of outfit for Sangeet.
Leave valuables at the hotel — things can get messy during Haldi!
The Sangeet has open seating — find your group and enjoy the performances.
Day 2 · Tuesday, 25 November

Vidhi, Wedding & Reception

9am
Until 5pm
🙏
Vidhi · 9:00 AM
Sacred Rituals

Traditional Hindu pre-wedding rituals performed with the priest. Sacred ceremonies that prepare and bless the couple for their new journey together.

🕘 9:00 AM · Please arrive by 8:45
💍
Wedding · 12:15 PM
The Wedding Ceremony

Sanket and Sneha exchange vows in a beautiful traditional ceremony. The most sacred moment of the two days — be seated by 12:00 PM.

🕛 12:15 PM · Be seated by noon
🥂
Reception · 2:00 PM
Wedding Reception

Celebrate the newlyweds! An afternoon of incredible food, toasts, photos, and dancing to close out this unforgettable two days.

🕑 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM
✨
What to wear — Day 2
Formal Indian or Western for both ceremony and reception. Saree, lehenga, or salwar suit for women. Sherwani, suit, or formal kurta for men. Bright colours are strongly encouraged — this is a celebration!

💡 Tips for Day 2

Arrive by 8:45 AM for Vidhi — the rituals begin promptly at 9:00 AM.
Be fully seated for the wedding ceremony by 12:00 PM.
Dress formally — this is the main wedding day. Bright, festive colours are encouraged!
Photos with the couple will happen after the ceremony and during reception.
Reception ends at 5:00 PM. Pre-book your return cab in advance.
